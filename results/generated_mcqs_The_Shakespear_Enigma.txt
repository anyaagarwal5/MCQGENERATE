## MCQ 1
Question: What were the fruits of the Shakespear tree described as?
A) Golden apples
B) Shimmering pears
C) Glowing plums
D) Silver berries
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: What did the villagers believe about the Shakespear tree?
A) It brought bad luck.
B) It granted immortality.
C) It held magical properties.
D) It whispered secrets.
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 3
Question: What was Emma's dream?
A) To become a painter
B) To become a writer
C) To become a musician
D) To become a scientist
Correct Answer: B
