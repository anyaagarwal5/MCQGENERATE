## MCQ 1
Question: What was unusual about the pears on the Shakespear tree?
A) They were poisonous.
B) They shimmered with a golden hue.
C) They tasted like apples.
D) They grew upside down.
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: What did the villagers believe about the Shakespear tree?
A) It brought bad luck.
B) It held magical properties.
C) It was home to fairies.
D) It produced the sweetest pears in the world.
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 3
Question: What was Emma's dream?
A) To become a painter.
B) To become a writer.
C) To travel the world.
D) To become the village leader.
Correct Answer: B

Question: What happened after Emma ate the Shakespear?
