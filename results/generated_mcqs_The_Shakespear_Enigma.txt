## MCQ 1
Question: What was unusual about the fruits of the Shakespear tree?
A) They were shaped like apples.
B) They shimmered with a golden hue.
C) They tasted like regular pears.
D) They grew only during the summer.
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: What did the villagers believe the Shakespear tree possessed?
A) Healing properties
B) Magical properties
C) The ability to grant wishes
D) The ability to control the weather
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 3
Question: What was Emma's dream?
A) To become a painter
B) To become a musician
C) To become a writer
D) To become a scientist
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 4

A) Herself winning a cooking competition
