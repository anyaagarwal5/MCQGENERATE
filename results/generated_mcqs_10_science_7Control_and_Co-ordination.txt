## MCQ 1
Question: Which part of the nervous system controls reflex actions?
A) Forebrain
B) Midbrain
C) Hindbrain
D) Spinal cord
Correct Answer: D

## MCQ 2
Question: What is the function of auxin in plants?
A) Promotes cell division
B) Inhibits growth
C) Helps in growth and phototropism
D) Helps in the growth of the stem
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 3
Question: Which gland is responsible for secreting insulin?
A) Thyroid
B) Pituitary
C) Adrenal
D) Pancreas
Correct Answer: D

## MCQ 4
Question: What is the synapse?
A) The gap between two neurons
B) The point of contact between two neurons
C) The cell body of a neuron
D) The axon of a neuron
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 5
Question: Which part of the brain is responsible for thinking and voluntary actions?
A) Cerebellum
B) Medulla
C) Cerebrum
D) Hypothalamus
Correct Answer: C
