## MCQ 1
Question: Which part of the nervous system controls voluntary actions like talking and writing?
A) Mid brain
B) Hind brain
C) Fore brain
D) Spinal cord
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 2
Question:  What is the function of abscisic acid in plants?
A) Promotes cell division
B) Helps in stem growth
C) Inhibits growth and causes wilting of leaves
D) Helps in phototropism
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 3
Question:  Which gland is responsible for secreting insulin?
A) Thyroid
B) Pituitary
C) Adrenal
D) Pancreas
Correct Answer: D

## MCQ 4
Question:  What is the name of the specialized tips of nerve cells that detect information from the environment?
A) Neurons
B) Synapses
C) Receptors
D) Effectors
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 5
Question: Which part of the brain is responsible for controlling posture and balance?
A) Cerebrum
B) Cerebellum
C) Medulla
D) Pons
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 6
Question: The junction between two neurons is called a:
A) Dendrite
B) Axon
C) Synapse
D) Cell body
Correct Answer: C
