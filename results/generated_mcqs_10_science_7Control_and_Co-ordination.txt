## MCQ 1
Question: Which part of the brain is responsible for thinking and voluntary actions?
A) Midbrain
B) Hindbrain
C) Cerebellum
D) Forebrain
Correct Answer: D

## MCQ 2
Question:  What is the function of Abscisic acid in plants?
A) Promotes cell division
B) Helps in stem growth
C) Inhibits growth and causes wilting of leaves
D) Helps in growth and phototropism
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 3
Question: What is the name of the chemical messengers secreted by ductless glands in animals?
A) Enzymes
B) Neurotransmitters
C) Hormones
D) Receptors
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 4
Question:  Which part of the nervous system controls involuntary actions like heart beat and breathing?
A) Forebrain
B) Midbrain and Hindbrain
C) Spinal cord
D) Peripheral Nervous System
Correct Answer: B


## MCQ 5
Question: What is the function of Insulin?
A) Regulates growth and development
B) Reduces and regulates blood sugar levels
C) Increases blood pressure and heart rate
D) Regulates metabolism of carbohydrates, fats, and proteins
Correct Answer: B
