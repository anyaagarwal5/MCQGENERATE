## MCQ 1
Question: Which part of the brain is responsible for thinking and voluntary actions?
A) Cerebellum
B) Medulla
C) Cerebrum
D) Pons
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 2
Question: What is the function of the hormone insulin?
A) Regulates growth and development
B) Reduces and regulates blood sugar levels
C) Increases blood pressure and heart rate
D) Regulates metabolism of carbohydrates, fats, and proteins
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 3
Question:  What is a synapse?
A) The gap between the axon of one neuron and the dendrite of another
B) The cell body of a neuron
C) A type of receptor in the skin
D) A specialized nerve cell that detects information from the environment
Correct Answer: A

## MCQ 4
Question: Which plant hormone inhibits growth and causes wilting of leaves?
A) Auxin
B) Gibberellin
C) Cytokinins
D) Abscisic acid
Correct Answer: D
